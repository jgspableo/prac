Unit Conversions
Temperature




To convert celsius to fahrenheit yourself, use this formula replacing the `C` with your temperature in celsius: (C × 9/5) + 32
Weight




To convert kilograms to pounds yourself, use this formula replacing the `K` with your weight in kilograms: K × 2.205
Distance




To convert kilometers to miles yourself, use this formula replacing the `K` with your distance in kilometers: K × 0.6214
